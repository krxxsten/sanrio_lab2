What Is Sanrio?

Founded in 1960, Sanrio is a Japanese company known for creating and licensing cute characters and designs often centered around themes of friendship and kindness. Some of their most commonly known characters are Hello Kitty, Keroppi, Kuromi, Cinnamaroll, and more! Using these characters, Sanrio has found success in creating products centered around the friendship of these characters; selling shirts, plushies, stationery items, and more.
